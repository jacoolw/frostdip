Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026

FrostDip is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Data Storage

All data you create in FrostDip, including session history, timer records, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. None of this data ever leaves your device.

No Data Collection

FrostDip does not collect any personal information, usage data, or device information. There are no user accounts and no sign-in required.

No Analytics

FrostDip does not use any analytics frameworks or tracking tools. We have no insight into how you use the app.

No Advertising

FrostDip contains no advertisements and does not integrate with any advertising networks.

No Third-Party Services

FrostDip does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
